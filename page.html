The "What Makes Our Childhood" project...

began with an idea sparked by a conversation my friends and I had while sharing jelly in class.
As we discussed our favorite childhood candies, I realized how cultural differences shape our experiences of childhood. This led me to delve deeper into childhood snacks, and even further into the memories from diaries we wrote as a child and the childhood literature. I hope this website helps you reflect on your own childhood and sparks a sense of nostalgia. Do you want to share anything sweet about your childhood? Fill out this survey!
